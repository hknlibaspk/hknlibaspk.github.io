Our Story

Crafted for Comfort,
Chosen for Style

HKN Libas PK was born from a simple belief — that premium Pakistani fashion shouldn't be complicated to find or hard to trust. We handpick every piece for its fabric, stitching and finish, so Ladies and Men across Pakistan can shop confidently without stepping into a showroom.

From breezy lawn suits for summer to rich khaddar and velvet for winter, every design in our collection is chosen to feel as good as it looks. No middlemen, no guesswork — just a quick WhatsApp message and your favorite outfit is on its way to your door.

We've also brought our love for design into the bedroom — our vivid 3D design bedsheets feature lifelike, richly detailed patterns on soft, fade-resistant fabric, giving your home the same premium touch as your wardrobe.

Our Promise

Return Policy

Your satisfaction matters to us. While we carefully check every piece before it's shipped, we understand that occasional design or quality issues can happen — and we've got you covered.

3 Day Return Window
  • ✓ Returns accepted within 3 days of delivery if there's a design, stitching or fabric defect.
  • ✓ Simply message us on WhatsApp with your order details and a photo of the issue — we'll sort it out quickly.
  • ✓ The item must be unused, unwashed, and in its original condition with tags intact.
  • ✓ Once approved, we'll happily arrange a free exchange for the correct item.
